Jobs

Want to be a part of the Hoplite team? View our current openings.

Our Team

The Hoplite team has been been working together for over a year now and we're looking to make things better every chance we get. Creating things and making them come to life is our passion, and we think anyone out there with a high drive to be a part of a Minecraft server and a desire to dedicate their work to our community has an absolute place on our team. All work is done remotely and pay depends on the position.

General Expectations

All applicants should meet the following base requirements:

  • Be able to speak and write English at a C1 fluency level
  • Have a working microphone and ability to speak in call
  • Be able to work during or adapt to US/UK working hours
  • Have some experience with working on a Minecraft server or large organized team
  • Have a dedicated interest in Hoplite and Minecraft servers in general
