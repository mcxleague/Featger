Server Rules

Playing on the server means that you automatically agree to follow the rules so that we can protect your experience and maintain the flow of the community. The rules are enforced by the staff members and they follow the moderation guidelines to carry out punishments.

Most of these rules are applicable across all of our processes. This includes the Minecraft and Discord server. The rules listed here are very generalized and do not cover every case scenario in specific detail. Should you break the rules, you may find yourself banned/muted for a more specific reason. Punishment times are determined by severity of offense and previous offenses that have been committed.

If you find someone you believe is breaking the rules or you would like to appeal a punishment, please create a support ticket on Discord.

Text and Voice Chat

All players are expected to carry themselves in text and voice chats. Text and voice chat rules have similar offenses, but some may be only applicable to text or voice chat specifically. Breaking voice chat rules will warrant a much more severe punishment.

  • Engaging in harassment of other players, including the use of offensive or obscene language targeted at another player is prohibited.
  • Spamming/flooding chats is prohibited.
  • Intentionally boosting microphone volume to unbearable levels in proximity chat is prohibited.
  • Discussion of controversial topics or anything that could be deemed as a disruption to the server environment is prohibited.

  • Use of discriminatory language is prohibited.
  • Targeting/Harassment of players based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristic is prohibited.

  • Promotion of other servers/third party websites is prohibited.
  • Promotion/linking of third party websites is prohibited.
  • **Phishing for Minecraft accounts/personal information of other players on the server, as well as the Hoplite Discord server, will result in a permanent blacklist - without chance of appeal.**

  • Sharing a player's personal information is strictly prohibited.
  • Threatening or carrying out the act of DDOSing is strictly prohibited.
  • Harassment involving any of player's personal information is strictly prohibited.

Gameplay Rules

Violation of most gameplay rules will result in a ban without warning.

  • Using modifications/cheats that provide an unfair advantage are prohibited (see blacklisted modification section below)
  • Abuse/Exploitation of any known bugs is prohibited.
  • Abuse/Exploitation of any server maps is prohibited.
  • Teaming/Partying with players that are using blacklisted modifications is prohibited.
  • All vanilla cave finding exploits are use at your own risk. Using them to locate players is strictly prohibited.
  • Reloading shaders to cavefind is prohibited.
  • Use of a mod to remove fog under lava is prohibited.
  • Use of a resource pack that reduces the opacity of lava is prohibited.
  • Strip mining is permitted.
  • Resource packs that outline ores for cosmetic purposes are permitted.
  • Use of fullbright/gamma correction mods/resource packs are permitted.

  • Crossteaming in all Battle Royale modes is prohibited.
  • Blockglitching into areas of the deathmatch map that other players are unable to access normally is prohibited.
  • Team griefing/sabotaging is prohibited.
  • Creation of inappropriate builds is prohibited.

  • Use of inappropriate skins or in game names is prohibited.
  • Harmful use/exploitation of server commands is prohibited.
  • Ban/punishment evasion is prohibited.
  • Certain violations of Account Misconduct will result in permanent bans without appeal.

Allowed Modifications

Here's a list of the allowed modifications on Hoplite. If you have any questions, feel free to ask a staff member in our Discord server!

  • Mods that improve the performance of the Minecraft client without making changes to the game itself are permitted.

  • Mods that adjust the Minecraft client's brightness and gamma settings are permitted.

  • Mods that alter the HUD, without providing extra information that would otherwise be inaccessible to the player are permitted. Examples of permitted mods include armor and status effect displays, which is information that is displayed in the inventory. Examples of mods NOT permitted include mini-maps that display player locations, enemy player health/armor indicators, and camera perspective mods that exceed the capabilities of the F5 camera function.

  • Mods that change the look of the game are permitted so long as they do not alter the core properties of blocks. For example, a resource pack that makes lava transparent would not be allowed, as the vanilla lava texture is opaque; this would be an unfair advantage. A shader pack that makes water more transparent is permitted, as the default water texture is also transparent, allowing both the vanilla and modded player the chance to spot some diamonds underneath water in a cave.

Blacklisted Modifications

Here's a list of the blacklisted modifications on Hoplite. If you have any questions, feel free to ask a staff member in our Discord server!

  • Any modification which provides any significant advantage to players in any gamemode is disallowed (ex. PvP hacked clients, X-Ray, health indicators). Additionally, any mod that alters the way in which the Minecraft client interacts with and communicates with the server is disallowed, regardless if they otherwise fit into an allowed category. We do not provide a list of disallowed mods, use your better judgement!
